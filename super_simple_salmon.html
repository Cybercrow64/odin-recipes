Super Simple Salmon

Description

Don't have time for a complex salmon recipe? Don't wanna spend forever making the recipe site with all the ingredients and steps? Super Simple Salmon to the rescue!

ingredients

  • 1 tsp garlic butter
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 6 ounce salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Stir together the garlic powder, basil, and salt in a small bowl; rub in equal amounts onto the salmon fillets.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook the salmon in the butter until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side. Serve each piece of salmon with a lemon wedge.
